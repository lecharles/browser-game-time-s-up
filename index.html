ğŸ® Game Overview

Time's Up! is a fast-paced word-guessing party game for two teams. Players race against a 60-second timer to help their teammates guess as many words as possible!

Setup

  1. Form two teams with 2-4 players each (teams must be equal size)
  2. Enter team names and player names
  3. Each player submits 3-5 words (people, places, things, phrases)

Gameplay

Round 1: Describe It ğŸ’¬

  • Describe the word using any words EXCEPT the word itself
  • Teams alternate turns
  • Each turn lasts 60 seconds
  • Click "Got It!" when your team guesses correctly (1 point + word removed)
  • Click "Skip" if you can't get the word (word stays in pool)

Round 2: One Word ğŸ”¤

  • Same words, new challenge!
  • Give only ONE single-word clue (not the word itself)
  • Same rules as Round 1
